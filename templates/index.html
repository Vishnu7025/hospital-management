TOUCHING HUMANS LIVES

"Wherever the art of medicine is loved, there is also a love of humanity."


Seeking medical treatment outside your home country can be a challenge for you and your family. Your search for the best medical treatment at a reasonable price will lead you to explore India and the hospitals there. As you explore further you would notice that the success rate for most of the medical surgeries are on par with the best in the world.


Medical history

A medical certificate that you can travel overseas for treatment has to be arranged which is signed by the local doctor. Share your medical case history with details of treatment taken, latest medical reports and current clinical status with the expert team at SK Hospitals. SK Hospital strictly follows medical confidentiality and the information discussed will remain with the team that is engaged in the discussion.

Online Consultation

Once the latest medical reports are shared, .an online consultation with the concerned doctor can be arranged. The doctor will suggest the options available and the most suitable treatment plan. SK Hospital will share the treatment plan with an estimate of likely charges

visa assistants

Once the travel date is confirmed, we will send the medical visa invitation letter. This letter is required to get the medical visa from the Indian embassy in your country. There is a possibility of extension of the medical visa duration if the treatment demands it. The International patient care team will attend to this. Foreign nationals staying in India for more than 180 days have to register at the FRRO (Foreigners Regional Registration Office) within 14 days of arrival. Hospital will provide assistance to the patient/bystanders to register at the FRRO.


Airport Pick up

On request, the International patient care team can arrange an airport pickup for you. We will share the name and contact details of the staff who will pick you up from the airport on arrival. The staff will escort you to the hospital or drop you at the hotel booked. You will have the choice of booking a hotel online before your medical trip to India starts. We can propose a few hotels based on your budget and proximity to the hospital.
